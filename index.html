How Paint Protection
Film Works

Stages

Paint protection film is made from a high-quality, transparent material that is applied directly to your car's paint. It is designed to absorb impacts, preventing scratches and chips from forming. The film also contains UV blockers, which prevent fading and discoloration caused by sunlight.

01

Application

PPF is applied to the vehicle's paint surface using a specialized process.

02

Adhesive

The film has a strong adhesive that bonds it to the paint, creating a barrier between the paint and the elements.

03

Thickness

PPF is available in various thicknesses (typically 3-5 mils) to provide different levels of protection.

04

Material

The film is made from a high-quality, transparent material (usually polyurethane or polyvinyl chloride) that is resistant to yellowing, cracking, and fading.

05

Protection

PPF protects the paint from scratches, chips, fading, chemical stains, weathering.
